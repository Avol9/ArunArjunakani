Description

Cameos were famous during the Victorian era, but have been around since Roman times.  The designs feature beautiful profiles of people, usually women, against a backdrop.  Fashion has seen a renewed interest in Cameos as vintage cameos are showing up on drop earrings, necklaces, bracelets and even rings.

ArtCarved brings a familial spin to this timeless piece with a mother/child image etched on our cameo.  Make it your own by adding mini-charms with the birth stone and initial of your child.
